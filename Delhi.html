1. Red Fort


Details

  1. Built by – Shah Jahan

  2. Entry Fee – Free for children under 15, ₹10 for Indians, ₹250 for foreigners

  3. Opening Hours – 9 AM to 6 PM, closed on Mondays

  4. Tips – Long queues during peak season

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Chandni Chowk

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 20 km

2. India Gate


Details

  1. Built by – Edwin Lutyens

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 24x7

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Central Secretariat

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 14 km

3. Qutub Minar


Details

  1. Built by – Qutb-ud-din Aibak

  2. Entry Fee – Free for children under 15, ₹10 for Indians, ₹250 for foreigners

  3. Opening Hours – 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM, closed on Mondays

  4. Tips – Popular despite past damage

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Qutub Minar

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 13.8 km (36 mins)

4. Humayun's Tomb


Details

  1. Built by – Empress Bega Begum

  2. Entry Fee – ₹10 for domestic & SAARC, ₹250 for others

  3. Opening Hours – 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM

  4. Tips – Often included in guided tours

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Jor Bagh

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 17 km (44 mins)

5. Akshardham Temple


Details

  1. Built by – BAPS

  2. Entry Fee – Free (separate charges for exhibitions)

  3. Opening Hours – 9:30 AM - 6:30 PM, closed on Mondays

  4. Tips – Magnificent views, visible from metro

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Akshardham

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 22.2 km (54 mins)

6. Chhatarpur Temple


Details

  1. Built by – Fariborz Sahba

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – Tue-Sun 9:30 AM – 7 PM (summer), 9:30 AM – 5 PM (winter)

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Kalkaji & Nehru Place

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 16.4 km (35 mins)

7. ISKCON Temple


Details

  1. Built by – Shah Jahan

  2. Entry Fee – Free (videography ₹300)

  3. Opening Hours – 7:00 AM – 12:00 PM; 1:30 PM – 6:30 PM

  4. Tips – Closed during prayers

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Chawri Bazaar

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 18 km (55 mins)

8. Lotus Temple


Details

  1. Built by – Fariborz Sahba

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – Tue-Sun 9:30 AM – 7 PM (summer), 9:30 AM – 5 PM (winter)

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Kalkaji & Nehru Place

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 16.4 km (35 mins)

9. Jama Masjid


Details

  1. Built by – Shah Jahan

  2. Entry Fee – Free (videography ₹300)

  3. Opening Hours – 7:00 AM – 12:00 PM; 1:30 PM – 6:30 PM

  4. Tips – Closed during prayers

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Chawri Bazaar

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 18 km (55 mins)

10. National Rail Museum


Details

  1. Entry Fee – ₹20

  2. Opening Hours – 9:30 AM – 5:30 PM, closed on Mondays & holidays

  3. Tips – Educative for kids

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Dhaula Kuan

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 8.1 km (21 mins)

11. Rashtrapati Bhavan


Details

  1. Built by – Sir Edwin Lutyens

  2. Opening Hours – 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM

  3. Tips – Entry requires pre-booking on official website

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Central Secretariat

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 12 km (27 mins)

12. Jantar Mantar


Details

  1. Built by – Maharaja Jai Singh II

  2. Entry Fee – ₹5

  3. Opening Hours – 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

  4. Tips – Hosts cultural events

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Janpath

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 13.6 km (29 mins)

13. Purana Qila


Details

  1. Built by – Sher Shah Suri

  2. Entry Fee – ₹20 for Indians, ₹100 for foreigners

  3. Opening Hours – 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM

  4. Tips – Boating nearby is popular

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Pragati Maidan

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 16.2 km (35 mins)

14. Gurudwara Bangla Sahib


Details

  1. Built by – General Sardar Bhagel Singh

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 24x7

  4. Tips – Visit after sunset as marble floor is hot

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Rajiv Chowk

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 13 km (28 mins)

15. Raj Ghat


Details

  1. Built by – Vanu G. Bhuta

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 10 AM – 5 PM, closed on Mondays

  4. Tips – Popular with school groups

  5. Nearest Metro Station – ITO

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 18.6 km (43 mins)

16. Hauz Khas Fort


Details

  1. Built by – Alauddin Khilji (14th century)

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 10:30 AM – 7 PM

  4. Tips – Enjoy live music with scenic views

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Green Park

  6. Distance from Delhi Airport – 11.6 km (25 mins)

17. Agrasen ki Baoli


Details

  1. Built by – Raja Agrasen

  2. Opening Hours – 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM

  3. Tips – Great for photography and selfies

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Barakhamba Road / Rajiv Chowk

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 16.5 km (35 mins)

18. Nehru Park


Details

  1. Entry Fee – Free

  2. Opening Hours – 6:00 AM – 8:00 PM

  3. Tips – Hosts cultural and musical events

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Lok Kalyan Marg / Jor Bagh

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 8.4 km (20 mins)

19. Craft Museum


Details

  1. Entry Fee – Free

  2. Opening Hours – 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM (July–Sept), 9:30 AM – 6:00 PM (Oct–June), closed on Mondays & national holidays

  3. Tips – Showcases unique craftsmanship

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Pragati Maidan

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 16.3 km (42 mins)

20. Chandni Chowk


Details

  1. Built by – Shah Jahan

  2. Opening Hours – 9:30 AM – 8:00 PM, closed on Sundays

  3. Tips – Perfect for wholesale shopping

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Chawri Bazaar

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 17.3 km (51 mins)

21. Sarojini Nagar Market


Details

  1. Opening Hours – 10:00 AM – 9:00 PM, closed on Mondays

  2. Tips – Bargain for clothes, accessories, and street shopping

  3. Nearest Metro Station – INA

  4. Distance from Delhi Airport – 9.9 km (27 mins)

22. Dilli Haat


Details

  1. Entry Fee – ₹20

  2. Opening Hours – 10:00 AM – 10:00 PM, daily

  3. Tips – Best for shopping, food, and souvenirs

  4. Nearest Metro Station – INA

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 10.2 km (29 mins)

23. National Museum


Details

  1. Built by – Ganesh Bhikaji Devolikar

  2. Entry Fee – ₹20 for Indians, ₹650 for foreigners

  3. Opening Hours – 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM, closed on Mondays

  4. Tips – Do not touch exhibits

  5. Nearest Metro Station – Udyog Bhavan

24. Khan Market


Details

  1. Opening Hours – 10:00 AM – 11:00 PM, closed Sunday

  2. Tips – Upscale crowd, sophisticated shopping

  3. Nearest Metro Station – Khan Market

25. Karol Bagh Market


Details

  1. Built by – Shuja-ud-Daula

  2. Entry Fee – ₹15 for Indians & SAARC, ₹200 for foreigners

  3. Opening Hours – 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM, daily

  4. Nearest Metro Station – Karol Bagh

  5. Distance from Delhi Airport – 14.1 km (33 mins)
