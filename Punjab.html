Top 5 Tourist Places in Punjab

1. Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib), Amritsar
2. Wagah Border, Amritsar
3. Jallianwala Bagh, Amritsar
4. Rock Garden, Chandigarh
5. Anandpur Sahib, Rupnagar

1. Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib) – Amritsar


Details

  1. Built by – Guru Arjan Dev Ji

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – Open 24 hours

  4. Tips – Cover your head, remove shoes, and avoid taking photos inside the sanctum

  5. Nearest Railway Station – Amritsar Junction (2 km)

  6. Nearest Airport – Shri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport (12 km)

  7. Travel Time – Approx. 25 minutes from airport

2. Wagah Border – Amritsar


Details

  1. Famous for – Beating Retreat Ceremony

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 10 AM to 4 PM (Ceremony starts around 4:30 PM)

  4. Tips – Arrive early for good seats, carry water, avoid large bags

  5. Nearest Railway Station – Amritsar Junction (30 km)

  6. Nearest Airport – Shri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport (35 km)

  7. Travel Time – Approx. 1 hour from Amritsar city

3. Jallianwala Bagh – Amritsar


Details

  1. Built in Memory of – Martyrs of the 1919 Massacre

  2. Entry Fee – Free

  3. Opening Hours – 6:30 AM to 7:30 PM

  4. Tips – Visit the museum for historical exhibits

  5. Nearest Railway Station – Amritsar Junction (2 km)

  6. Nearest Airport – Shri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport (13 km)

  7. Travel Time – Approx. 20 minutes from airport
