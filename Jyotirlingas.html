12 Jyotirlingas in India

1. Somnath – Veraval, Gujarat
2. Mallikarjuna – Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh
3. Mahakaleshwar – Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh
4. Omkareshwar – Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh
5. Kedarnath – Kedarnath, Uttarakhand
6. Bhimashankar – Pune, Maharashtra
7. Kashi Vishwanath – Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
8. Trimbakeshwar – Nashik, Maharashtra
9. Vaidyanath – Deoghar, Jharkhand / Parli, Maharashtra
10. Nageshwar – Dwarka, Gujarat
11. Ramanathaswamy – Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
12. Grishneshwar – Ellora, Maharashtra

1. Somnath – Veraval, Gujarat


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Veraval Railway Station

  2. Nearest Airport – Diu Airport (95 km)

  3. Travel Time – 2–3 hours by car (₹1500–₹2000)

  4. Opening Hours – 6 AM to 9 PM

  5. Tips – Visit during evening Aarti for best view

2. Mallikarjuna – Srisailam, Andhra Pradesh


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Markapur Road

  2. Nearest Airport – Hyderabad (213 km)

  3. Travel Time – 5–6 hours by road (₹1500–₹2000)

  4. Opening Hours – 6 AM–12 PM, 3 PM–8 PM

  5. Tips – Use the ropeway for easy temple access

3. Mahakaleshwar – Ujjain, Madhya Pradesh


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Ujjain Junction

  2. Nearest Airport – Indore (57 km)

  3. Travel Time – 1–1.5 hours (₹500–₹700)

  4. Opening Hours – 4 AM to 11 PM

  5. Tips – Don’t miss the Bhasma Aarti in the morning

4. Omkareshwar – Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Omkareshwar Road

  2. Nearest Airport – Indore (77 km)

  3. Travel Time – 1.5–2 hours (₹700–₹1000)

  4. Opening Hours – 5 AM to 10 PM

  5. Tips – Take a boat ride to Mandhata Island

5. Kedarnath – Uttarakhand


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Rishikesh

  2. Nearest Airport – Dehradun (239 km)

  3. Travel Mode – Trekking or helicopter (₹5000–₹6000)

  4. Opening Months – May to November

  5. Tips – Carry warm clothes; check weather updates

6. Bhimashankar – Pune, Maharashtra


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Pune Junction

  2. Nearest Airport – Pune Airport (110 km)

  3. Travel Time – 3–4 hours (₹1500–₹2000)

  4. Opening Hours – 6 AM to 8 PM

  5. Tips – Located amidst dense forests; great for nature lovers

7. Kashi Vishwanath – Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Varanasi Junction

  2. Nearest Airport – Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport (22 km)

  3. Travel Time – 30–45 mins (₹200–₹300)

  4. Opening Hours – 4 AM to 11 PM

  5. Tips – Morning Ganga Aarti is must-watch

8. Trimbakeshwar – Nashik, Maharashtra


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Nashik Road

  2. Nearest Airport – Nashik Airport (28 km)

  3. Travel Time – 1 hour (₹300–₹500)

  4. Opening Hours – 5 AM to 10 PM

  5. Tips – Known for unique three-faced Shiva Lingam

9. Vaidyanath – Deoghar, Jharkhand


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Jasidih Junction

  2. Nearest Airport – Deoghar Airport (14 km)

  3. Travel Time – 30–40 mins (₹200–₹300)

  4. Opening Hours – 5 AM to 10 PM

  5. Tips – Very crowded in Sawan month

10. Nageshwar – Dwarka, Gujarat


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Dwarka Railway Station

  2. Nearest Airport – Jamnagar Airport (128 km)

  3. Travel Time – 2–3 hours (₹1500–₹2000)

  4. Opening Hours – 5 AM to 10 PM

  5. Tips – Located near Dwarka beach temples

11. Ramanathaswamy – Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Rameswaram Station

  2. Nearest Airport – Madurai Airport (170 km)

  3. Travel Time – 3–4 hours (₹1000–₹1500)

  4. Opening Hours – 5 AM–1 PM, 3 PM–9 PM

  5. Tips – Famous for 22 holy water tanks

12. Grishneshwar – Ellora, Maharashtra


Details

  1. Nearest Railway Station – Aurangabad

  2. Nearest Airport – Aurangabad Airport (30 km)

  3. Travel Time – 45 mins–1 hour (₹300–₹500)

  4. Opening Hours – 6 AM to 9 PM

  5. Tips – Located near Ellora Caves, beautiful architecture
