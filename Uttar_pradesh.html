Top 7 Tourist Places in Uttar Pradesh

1. Taj Mahal, Agra
2. Varanasi (Kashi)
3. Agra Fort, Agra
4. Mathura & Vrindavan
5. Fatehpur Sikri, Agra
6. Ayodhya
7. Sarnath (Near Varanasi)

1. Taj Mahal – Agra


Details

  1. Built by: Emperor Shah Jahan

  2. Entry Fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹1100 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6:30 PM, closed on Fridays

  4. Tips: Best visited during sunrise or sunset for the best view

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Agra Cantt

  6. Nearest Airport: Agra Airport

  7. Travel Time: Approx. 30 minutes from Airport

2. Varanasi (Kashi) – Varanasi


Details

  1. Famous for: Kashi Vishwanath Temple & Ganga Aarti

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 4 AM – 11 PM

  4. Tips: Attend the evening Ganga Aarti for a spiritual experience

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Varanasi Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport

  7. Travel Time: Around 30 minutes from Airport

3. Agra Fort – Agra


Details

  1. Built by: Emperor Akbar

  2. Entry Fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹650 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Hire a local guide to understand the historical background

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Agra Fort Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Agra Airport

  7. Travel Time: 20–25 minutes from Airport

4. Mathura & Vrindavan


Details

  1. Famous for: Birthplace of Lord Krishna

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 5 AM – 10 PM

  4. Tips: Visit during Janmashtami or Holi for grand celebrations

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Mathura Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Agra Airport

  7. Travel Time: Around 1 hour from Agra

5. Fatehpur Sikri – Agra


Details

  1. Built by: Emperor Akbar

  2. Entry Fee: ₹35 (Indians), ₹550 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Carry drinking water; the area is quite large

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Fatehpur Sikri Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Agra Airport

  7. Travel Time: Around 45 minutes from Agra city

6. Ayodhya


Details

  1. Famous for: Ram Janmabhoomi Temple

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 9 PM

  4. Tips: Visit during Ram Navami for special rituals and decorations

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Ayodhya Dham Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Maharishi Valmiki International Airport, Ayodhya

  7. Travel Time: 20–25 minutes from Airport

7. Sarnath – Near Varanasi


Details

  1. Famous for: The place where Lord Buddha delivered his first sermon

  2. Entry Fee: ₹20 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Don’t miss the Archaeological Museum nearby

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Varanasi Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Lal Bahadur Shastri Airport

  7. Travel Time: Around 30 minutes from Varanasi city
