Top 7 Tourist Places in Gujarat

1. Somnath Temple, Veraval
2. Rann of Kutch, Bhuj
3. Gir National Park, Junagadh
4. Dwarkadhish Temple, Dwarka
5. Sabarmati Ashram, Ahmedabad
6. Statue of Unity, Kevadia
7. Saputara Hill Station, Dang

1. Somnath Temple – Veraval


Details

  1. Built by: Chalukya Kings / Reconstruction by Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 5 AM – 10 PM

  4. Tips: Visit during evening Aarti for a spiritual experience

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Veraval Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Diu Airport (approx. 100 km)

  7. Travel Time: 2–3 hours from nearest airport

2. Rann of Kutch – Bhuj


Details

  1. Famous for: White Salt Desert and Rann Utsav Festival

  2. Entry Fee: Free (Festival entry may vary)

  3. Opening Hours: Sunrise to Sunset; best visited November – February

  4. Tips: Carry warm clothes at night; plan accommodation in advance during Rann Utsav

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Bhuj Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Bhuj Airport

  7. Travel Time: Around 30–40 min from Bhuj city

3. Gir National Park – Junagadh


Details

  1. Famous for: Asiatic Lions

  2. Entry Fee: ₹250 (Indians), ₹500 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 5 PM (Safari timings may vary)

  4. Tips: Book safari tickets in advance; carry binoculars

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Junagadh Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Rajkot Airport (approx. 120 km)

  7. Travel Time: 2–3 hours from Rajkot Airport

4. Dwarkadhish Temple – Dwarka


Details

  1. Built by: Yadava Dynasty (Renovated several times)

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 4 AM – 10 PM

  4. Tips: Attend evening Aarti for best experience; follow dress code for temple

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Dwarka Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Porbandar Airport (approx. 130 km)

  7. Travel Time: Around 2–3 hours from Porbandar Airport

5. Sabarmati Ashram – Ahmedabad


Details

  1. Famous for: Residence of Mahatma Gandhi

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 8:30 AM – 7 PM

  4. Tips: Visit museum and Gandhi memorial; photography allowed outside

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Ahmedabad Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport

  7. Travel Time: 15–20 min from airport

6. Statue of Unity – Kevadia


Details

  1. Built in honor of: Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

  2. Entry Fee: ₹120–₹350 depending on viewing gallery (Indians), ₹400–₹600 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 8 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Visit early morning for lesser crowd; book tickets online

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Vadodara Junction (approx. 90 km)

  6. Nearest Airport: Vadodara Airport

  7. Travel Time: 2–3 hours from Vadodara

7. Saputara Hill Station – Dang


Details

  1. Famous for: Hill station, lakes, and tribal culture

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: Open all day

  4. Tips: Best visited during monsoon or winter; boating available in the lake

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Waghai Railway Station (approx. 50 km)

  6. Nearest Airport: Surat Airport (approx. 180 km)

  7. Travel Time: 4–5 hours from Surat Airport
