Top 7 Tourist Places in Madhya Pradesh

1. Khajuraho Temples, Chhatarpur
2. Kanha National Park, Mandla
3. Gwalior Fort, Gwalior
4. Ujjain (Mahakaleshwar Temple)
5. Sanchi Stupa, Raisen
6. Pachmarhi Hill Station
7. Bandhavgarh National Park, Umaria

1. Khajuraho Temples – Chhatarpur


Details

  1. Built by: Chandela Dynasty

  2. Entry Fee: ₹40 (Indians), ₹600 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Best visited in the early morning; photography allowed outside temples

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Khajuraho Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Khajuraho Airport

  7. Travel Time: 10–15 minutes from Airport

2. Kanha National Park – Mandla


Details

  1. Famous for: Bengal Tigers and wildlife safari

  2. Entry Fee: ₹300 (Indians), ₹1500 (Foreigners) per safari

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM; safari timings vary

  4. Tips: Book safari tickets in advance; carry binoculars and camera

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Jabalpur Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Jabalpur Airport

  7. Travel Time: 3–4 hours from Jabalpur

3. Gwalior Fort – Gwalior


Details

  1. Built by: Raja Man Singh Tomar

  2. Entry Fee: ₹25 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Hire a guide to explore the fort thoroughly; visit early morning

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Gwalior Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Gwalior Airport

  7. Travel Time: 15–20 minutes from airport

4. Ujjain – Mahakaleshwar Temple


Details

  1. Famous for: One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: 4 AM – 12 PM & 4 PM – 10 PM

  4. Tips: Attend early morning Bhasma Aarti for spiritual experience

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Ujjain Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport, Indore

  7. Travel Time: Approx. 1.5 hours from Indore Airport

5. Sanchi Stupa – Raisen


Details

  1. Built by: Emperor Ashoka (3rd century BCE)

  2. Entry Fee: ₹40 (Indians), ₹500 (Foreigners)

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM

  4. Tips: Explore the museum nearby; best visited during cooler months

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Sanchi Railway Station / Bhopal Junction

  6. Nearest Airport: Raja Bhoj Airport, Bhopal

  7. Travel Time: Around 1–1.5 hours from Bhopal Airport

6. Pachmarhi Hill Station


Details

  1. Famous for: Hill station, waterfalls, caves, and wildlife

  2. Entry Fee: Free

  3. Opening Hours: Open all day

  4. Tips: Best visited in winter and monsoon; trekking and boating available

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Pipariya Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Bhopal Airport

  7. Travel Time: Approx. 3–4 hours from Bhopal Airport

7. Bandhavgarh National Park – Umaria


Details

  1. Famous for: Bengal Tigers and wildlife safari

  2. Entry Fee: ₹350 (Indians), ₹1500 (Foreigners) per safari

  3. Opening Hours: 6 AM – 6 PM; safari timings vary

  4. Tips: Book safari tickets in advance; best visited in winter

  5. Nearest Railway Station: Umaria Railway Station

  6. Nearest Airport: Jabalpur Airport (approx. 160 km)

  7. Travel Time: 3–4 hours from Jabalpur Airport
